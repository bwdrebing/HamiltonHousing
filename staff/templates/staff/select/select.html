Selection

Place a student in a room, suite, or apartment.

Select a room

Place students in a room.

For example, place a student in Macintosh 201, a double, with his/her friend.

Select a suite

Place students in a suite. For use during the blocking lottery.

For example, place Block 10 in Babbitt 30.

Select an apartment

Place students in an apartment (a room that pulls more than one other room).

For example, place a student and that student's friends in Anderson Road A, an apartment comprised of 4 rooms.
