FAQ

Q: What is RoomService?

Answer

The basic goal of RoomService is to provide information about which rooms are available, in real time, during Hamilton's housing lotteries. It provides up to date information about residence halls and their rooms. RoomService does not provide any way for students to select a room - all of the lotteries must still be attended in person.

Q: How do I use RoomService?

Answer

To view available rooms by building, select a building from the sidebar on the left (accessible by pressing the menu button in the upper left corner). To view all available rooms, select "View all rooms" from the sidebar. Building pages display a list of all available rooms and apartments, as well as floor plans.
